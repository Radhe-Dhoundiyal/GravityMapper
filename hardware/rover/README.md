# Rover

Mechanical design and assembly documentation for the GADV mobile platform.

## Platform Overview

The rover is a wheeled ground vehicle designed to carry the ESP32 sensor node over survey transects. Its key requirements are:

- Low vibration to minimise mechanical noise in accelerometer readings
- GPS antenna with clear sky view
- Minimum 2-hour battery endurance per survey session
- Ability to traverse grass, gravel, and compacted soil terrain

## Contents

- CAD files (FreeCAD / STEP format) for chassis and sensor mounting bracket
- Motor driver configuration for the ESP32 motor control firmware
- Bill of materials (BOM) for the mechanical assembly
